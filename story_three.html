Possible Dreams


Jump into the highest sky.

Perhaps you’ll find a space to sail.

Perhaps you’ll reach a maximum peak.

Perhaps you’ll become one of those potent fowl.


Jump into the highest sky.

Perhaps they are most likely to haul you up.

Perhaps there are hidden treads primed for you.

Perhaps invisible hands are stretched for you.


Jump and see.

Better a bird than a tree.

Better a taste than a thirst.

It’s possible all these can be.

Why not try and see?
