A New Jenkins Roberts


A leader shall rise again with honor, not in vain

Love and trust will he need

For he shall never walk in greed

A leader shall rise indeed, whose reign shall be clothed in welfarism

Whose tenure shall as limitless as the sky be

For he shall never walk in guile

His love for a country shall sturdy be.

His dreams of old shall confront the rains.

The message of change shall embrace his reign

For he shall never walk in pride.

Many shall be fain of his reign.

Many shall uplift his prestigious throne.

All shall ever brag in his deeds.

Truly! Truly! A new Jenkins Roberts he shall be.
