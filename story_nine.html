The Path

It’s a lonely destiny – but I don’t seem to fear

Walk and walk, this time I must. I’ve searched for men but shadows have!

Seen – how am I alone on this unstable path?


I ran as fast as the mind

Looking back felt like a forbidden trend

Then I sat a while for a while

Staring the night stars I began to cry

Why has hope shunned me?

Why has fear turned me a skinny bone?


Morning has faded – yet memories abide

But life has taken me as far as the inner core

Through a succession of pains that troubled my soul

But the wheels of faith brought me faith

Now, life has taken its linear path.


Destiny path I walk upon

Fear has left – doubt yet to flee

The light of this path is the light indeed

Verily! Verily I am not alone. For comfort I have who’s always with me
