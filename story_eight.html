Beautiful Liberia

Another place

Where mountains breed forests and streams!

Where monkey, raccoons, and lions feast!


Another place

Beyond blissful scenes!

Where gusty winds and mighty rains do engage

Where the utmost sun descends to parade with stars abaft


Another place

A blessed realm

Far far beyond – in westem lands

Where shadow neither stay nor dissuade the light


Another place

Where hope sustains all mint and splendor

Where dearest bears and stillness crisps


Another place

Where flairs are forged and reveries nailed

Where visions are perky; where freedom is unveiled


Another place

The strangest of race

Scenic and dazzling

Unruffled and fetching

In deed the noblest of all stars
