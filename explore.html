The Wish Of Bond

I wish to be just anything Beyond what I see!

That life will be a just haven for me

So all the world can see authentic beauty in me


I wish to be just a spirited being

Beyond all galaxies

That life will be an endless encounter for me

So the world can see absolute reality in me!

Reasonable Fight

Hope to get.

Little or much.

Faith to fight.

Failures as such.

Love to remain tight till a kindy touch.

No! No! I meant.

It takes faith to reach a height.

It takes love to be a light.

Whatever that height or faith

Learn to remain right

Even at darkest nights

Loose not they precious sight

To take time to think twice the time

For the future is inevitably bright.

The Path

It’s a lonely destiny – but I don’t seem to fear

Walk and walk, this time I must. I’ve searched for men but shadows have!

Seen – how am I alone on this unstable path?


I ran as fast as the mind

Looking back felt like a forbidden trend

Then I sat a while for a while

Staring the night stars I began to cry

Why has hope shunned me?

Why has fear turned me a skinny bone?


Morning has faded – yet memories abide

But life has taken me as far as the inner core

Through a succession of pains that troubled my soul

But the wheels of faith brought me faith

Now, life has taken its linear path.


Destiny path I walk upon

Fear has left – doubt yet to flee

The light of this path is the light indeed

Verily! Verily I am not alone. For comfort I have who’s always with me

Possible Dreams

Jump into the highest sky.

Perhaps you’ll find a space to sail.

Perhaps you’ll reach a maximum peak.

Perhaps you’ll become one of those potent fowl.


Jump into the highest sky.

Perhaps they are most likely to haul you up.

Perhaps there are hidden treads primed for you.

Perhaps invisible hands are stretched for you.


Jump and see.

Better a bird than a tree.

Better a taste than a thirst.

It’s possible all these can be.

Why not try and see?

The Light Path

Know your limits.

Be yourself.

Express your secrets;

Carefully lest you tell nemesis.

Keep tight your mouth;

But echo the truth out loud.

Faithfully look to God,

Patiently let go of your world.

Sit in silence and think,

Knowledge is dearest beloved.

Be confident – be patient,

Things can smoothly change.

Hope for much – expect little,

Life is a story none can precisely tell.

    My Expression

    Things will change

    Never will they remain the same

    Even if life was a game

    The need for change will always be


    The world has never known

    The deepest secret of its tone

    Though the world many have claimed to own

    Few shall forever search in vain


    History has come to pass

    Future yet difficult to realize!

    Countries shall desperately fight for the sky

    Though it’s just a matter of time


    I don’t know how to fly

    But at least l can try

    I must run day by day, to push up high

    So one day I can reach the highest sky

    Condemned Innocence

    Voices of old,

    The victims of wars!

    Sheltered with innocence;

    Bonded in tears!

    Sleepless In consciousness,

    Restless in death!


    Voices of old have risen from death:

    To strike shady stars that yet breath.

    To strike the sun that shines amid guilty crimes!

    Same that gifted the earth with guiltless souls!

    And ruined the peace of innocent lives!


    Voices of old,

    The victims of wars!

    Reasoned with death to shortly stay alive.

    To plead justice for their ancient souls.


    If guiltless souls should tarry for dawn,

    Then justice indeed must sing her song!

    Lest innocent lives be walked upon!

    Lest the lands see a sky on more.

    Lest the future from tallest heights descend.

    For justice for all is justice to all;

    Voices of old,

    The victims of wars!

    Real Being

    A real being

    Is one eyes can’t see.

    It exists in deed,

    Beyond what minds conceive.


    A real being

    Is God alone.

    Though he exists indeed,

    Him no flesh has seen.


    A real being

    Is one hands can’t feel.

    Faith, it takes to believe,

    What eyes can’t perceive.

      Beautiful Liberia

      Another place

      Where mountains breed forests and streams!

      Where monkey, raccoons, and lions feast!


      Another place

      Beyond blissful scenes!

      Where gusty winds and mighty rains do engage

      Where the utmost sun descends to parade with stars abaft


      Another place

      A blessed realm

      Far far beyond – in westem lands

      Where shadow neither stay nor dissuade the light


      Another place

      Where hope sustains all mint and splendor

      Where dearest bears and stillness crisps


      Another place

      Where flairs are forged and reveries nailed

      Where visions are perky; where freedom is unveiled


      Another place

      The strangest of race

      Scenic and dazzling

      Unruffled and fetching

      In deed the noblest of all stars

      The Struggle To Freedom

      Birthed into slavery and pain

      Imperiled to another me decrees. She couldn’t taste the brightness of a day

      Nor could she inherit the wealth of her land

      She grew in a society filled with terror and deceits

      A society that disavowed its culture and name

      Yet she did not shrink


      She hoped for beauty to sprout from her soil

      Day by day she couldn’t retain her faint tears

      Day by day she couldn’t spare her eyes witness the atrocities of her clan

      As their native lips were silenced for fear

      As the drought of despondency overshadowed her land

      “why must this be” she said “why must a glorious sun descend to scorch a tender plant out of it’s sail”


      “why must this be!” she cried. For these, she couldn’t sit in pretense

      She stood amid what seemed like an unconquerable force

      She stood against the mighty decrees, against the slavery and pain! And fought strong to preserve the rights of her land!


      Time upon time

      All battle were conquered and won!

      Time upon time

      She stood a first patriot to her land!

      And like a shooting star, she thrust through the aggressive bands!

      Nailing lasting freedom to her land.

      The Renaissance

      Everyday realty

      Brings us past days history.

      The struggle for freedom,

      Was a sign for our union.


      From Jenkins Roberts’ days,

      The stage has been set,

      To engage,

      And live together in harmony and respect.


      Decades ago,

      We fought ourselves,

      About things that concerned us.


      Despite the origin,

      We’re chained to one heritage.

      Despite the history,

      We can stand to make the best story.


      If there’s a little greed,

      There’s an urgent need,

      That we agree,

      To give heed,

      Lest the nation suffers grief!


      A nation,

      That stands against hatred,

      Is sure to obtain victory.


      Thus the time,

      The time to believe

      And embrace the need for change.


      Thus the dawn,

      The dawn to sacrifice,

      for the change that we need,

      To sustain the peace,

      For the nation to succeed.

      A New Jenkins Roberts

      A leader shall rise again with honor, not in vain

      Love and trust will he need

      For he shall never walk in greed

      A leader shall rise indeed, whose reign shall be clothed in welfarism

      Whose tenure shall as limitless as the sky be

      For he shall never walk in guile

      His love for a country shall sturdy be.

      His dreams of old shall confront the rains.

      The message of change shall embrace his reign

      For he shall never walk in pride.

      Many shall be fain of his reign.

      Many shall uplift his prestigious throne.

      All shall ever brag in his deeds.

      Truly! Truly! A new Jenkins Roberts he shall be.

            Button Bone

            One sunny day at Paynesville Town,

            Old man Joma was passing by.

            “ Button Bone’’ spotted his skinny thighs,

            Which everyone has been talking about.


            “ Ha ha ha’’ Button Bone insists to laugh,

            At least his friends were laughing too.

            Thus old man Joma turned around,

            To see the faces that made him mad.


            Then he saw the “ Button Bone’’,

            The famous of all, atleast he was told.

            Soon he saw the teenage child, his spirit alone went paralyzed.

            Cause “ Button Bone’’ as he was called:

            Is old man Joma own step child!

            The one he has planned to throw outside.


            When “ Button Bone” saw his pa,

            Like a child that can not hear,

            He knew for sure his end was there.
